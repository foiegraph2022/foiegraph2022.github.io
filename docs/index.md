# Program

Workshop will be held in person at [Pavillon André-Aisenstadt](https://goo.gl/maps/U2hRH9Z3RXM2aGEBA), Université de Montréal, rooms 1177 and 1355. 

<br>
Students have a chance to give a short talk about their work (10-12 minutes), which will be followed by Q&A (3-5 minutes).
